Cheesecake

Whenever I’m in New York, I make the trip to Junior’s in Brooklyn. The place is iconic, and the cheesecake is out of this world. Unlike your typical cheesecake, they use a lighter than air sponge for the crust. It’s the best complement to the velvety, rich, dense cheesecake filling. Make sure your base is cooked and cooled before adding the filling and always use a water bath!
